Hercules

Hercules is one of Greek God Zeus and mortal Alcemene. He was a hero but didn't have any friends because he would always make a mess of things. Hercules was trained by Delphi. He had to do a lot of training in order to do his 12 labers.

Hercules 12 laborers

1. To Kill Nemean Lion

2. To Kill Hydra

3. To Capture Cerynitian Hind

4. To Capture Erymanthian Boar

5. Clean the Stables of Augeius

6. Drive away Stymphalian Birds

7. Bring back Cretan Bull Knossos

8. Bring Back the Mares Diomedes

9. Bring Back Hippolyte's Girdle

10. Bring Back Cattle Geryon King of Cadiz

11. Bring Back Golden Apples of Hesperides

12. Bring Cerberus the Guard Dog of the Underworld
